# 🎯 CareerPath AI – Intelligent Career Guidance Platform

## 📌 Overview

**CareerPath AI** is an AI-assisted career guidance platform developed to help students, graduates, and aspiring professionals discover career paths that align with their interests, skills, personality, and long-term goals. Choosing the right career can be overwhelming due to the vast number of options available and the lack of personalized guidance. CareerPath AI addresses this challenge by providing intelligent career recommendations through an interactive assessment and data-driven insights.

The platform evaluates users based on their interests, technical skills, academic background, preferred work style, and career aspirations. Using AI-assisted recommendation techniques, it suggests suitable career paths along with detailed information such as required skills, educational qualifications, salary expectations, industry demand, career growth opportunities, and recommended learning resources.

Beyond recommendations, CareerPath AI acts as a complete career planning solution by generating personalized learning roadmaps that help users bridge skill gaps and prepare for their chosen profession. The project demonstrates the practical implementation of Artificial Intelligence concepts, full-stack web development, and modern UI/UX design while solving a real-world problem faced by millions of students worldwide.

# 💡 Problem Statement

Many students struggle to choose the right career because they lack personalized guidance and reliable information about different professions. Traditional career counseling is often expensive, time-consuming, or unavailable to many individuals.

CareerPath AI solves this problem by providing an intelligent platform that analyzes a user's interests and skills to recommend suitable career paths. Instead of generic advice, users receive personalized recommendations along with structured learning plans that help them confidently work toward their professional goals.

# ⚙️ How It Works

1. Users create an account and access the platform.
2. Complete an interactive career assessment questionnaire.
3. The system analyzes the user's responses.
4. AI-assisted algorithms evaluate interests, strengths, and preferences.
5. Personalized career recommendations are generated.
6. Users explore detailed career information.
7. The platform identifies missing skills.
8. Customized learning roadmaps are created.
9. Users receive recommended certifications and learning resources.
10. Progress can be tracked as users continue developing their skills.
